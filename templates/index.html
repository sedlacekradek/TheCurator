About The Curator

Welcome art lovers! The Curator is a web app that shows you paintings provided via the API of The Metropolitan Museum of Art. You can ask The Curator basic questions regarding the shown piece such as the name of the author, title or dimensions. Also, feel free to submit your reviews of the art, The Curator will be more than happy to read them and through automatic text recognition evaluate how much you like the art and how subjective your review seems to him.
