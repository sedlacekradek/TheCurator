About The Curator

Welcome art lovers! The Curator is a web app that shows you paintings provided via API of The Metropolitan Museum of Art. You can ask The Curator basic questions regarding the shown piece of art such as the name of the author, title or dimensions. Also, feel free to submit your reviews of the art, The Curator will be more than happy to read them and through automatic text recognition let you know how much he believes you like the painting and how much he thinks you are being objective in your review.
